Our Teachers

Shiekh Muhammad Al-Luhaidan

Sheikh Muhammad Al-Luhaidan is a respected Saudi imam and Quran reciter known for his emotional, heartfelt recitations of the Quran. He has gained significant recognition for his unique voice and mastery of tajweed, drawing listeners worldwide. Sheikh Al-Luhaidan often leads prayers in prominent mosques in Saudi Arabia and has also participated in various international events, showcasing his profound dedication to the art of Quranic recitation. His recitations are widely shared and appreciated for their ability to inspire reflection and spiritual connection among listeners.

Shiekh Salman Al-Utaybi

Sheikh Salman Al-Utaybi is a distinguished Saudi Quran reciter, widely respected for his clear, resonant voice and meticulous application of tajweed principles. His recitations are known for their balance of strength and tranquility, capturing the hearts of listeners and fostering a deep sense of spiritual reflection. Leading prayers in prominent mosques across Saudi Arabia, Sheikh Al-Utaybi has developed a strong following among those seeking an inspiring and reflective recitation experience. His recordings are circulated globally, inviting listeners into a more profound connection with the Quran's message.

Shiekh Mishary Al-Afasy

Mishary Al-Afasy is a prominent Kuwaiti Quran reciter, imam, and singer, celebrated for his captivating voice and exceptional talent in Quranic recitation. Known for his emotional depth and adherence to the rules of tajweed, Al-Afasy has gained a global following through his beautiful recitations, which resonate with listeners from diverse backgrounds. He has served as an imam in various mosques and has participated in numerous international Quranic competitions and events. In addition to his Quran recitation, Al-Afasy has released several albums that blend Quranic verses with a melodious voice, further enhancing his appeal and outreach in promoting the message of the Quran.
