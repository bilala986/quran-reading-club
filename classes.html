Qur'aan Classes: How they will be organised

Introduction

Our Qur'aan classes are thoughtfully designed to make memorizing, understanding, and connecting with the Qur'aan an enriching journey for everyone involved. No matter your current level or familiarity with the Qur'aan, our classes provide a supportive and structured environment to ensure consistent progress and meaningful learning.

Class Levels

We offer three levels to accommodate all students:

  • Beginner: Perfect for those starting their Qur'aan journey or seeking a foundation in recitation. We'll focus on Arabic pronunciation, basic tajweed rules, and familiarization with shorter surahs.
  • Intermediate: For students who have basic recitation skills and want to deepen their understanding and memorization. At this level, we focus on longer passages, applying tajweed rules more rigorously, and starting the memorization journey for key surahs.
  • Advanced: Tailored for students with a strong foundation in Qur'aanic recitation and tajweed. These classes delve into more challenging memorization goals, revision techniques, and preparation for independent memorization.

Class Structure

Each session is structured to include:

  • Warm-Up and Reflection:: Perfect for those starting their Qur'aan journey or seeking a foundation in recitation. We'll focus on Arabic pronunciation, basic tajweed rules, and familiarization with shorter surahs.
  • Recitation Practice: For students who have basic recitation skills and want to deepen their understanding and memorization. At this level, we focus on longer passages, applying tajweed rules more rigorously, and starting the memorization journey for key surahs.
  • Memorization Techniques: Tailored for students with a strong foundation in Qur'aanic recitation and tajweed. These classes delve into more challenging memorization goals, revision techniques, and preparation for independent memorization.
  • Review and Discussion: Tailored for students with a strong foundation in Qur'aanic recitation and tajweed. These classes delve into more challenging memorization goals, revision techniques, and preparation for independent memorization.

Weekly and Monthly Goals

Setting and achieving goals is a key part of our program:

  • Weekly Goals: Each student is encouraged to set a manageable memorization goal for the week. These goals will be reviewed in class, and support will be provided to overcome any difficulties encountered along the way.
  • Monthly Review Sessions: At the end of each month, we hold a review session to consolidate what has been learned. This includes a full revision of all previously memorized sections and group recitation practices to reinforce retention.
  • Advanced: Tailored for students with a strong foundation in Qur'aanic recitation and tajweed. These classes delve into more challenging memorization goals, revision techniques, and preparation for independent memorization.

Additional Features

To ensure all students feel motivated and supported, we offer:

  • One-on-One Sessions: Our instructors provide personalized attention and support to each student through optional one-on-one sessions, where students can receive additional guidance on specific areas.
  • Progress Tracking: Each student’s progress is tracked, allowing both instructors and students to monitor achievements and identify areas needing extra focus.
  • Community Support Groups: Beyond classes, students can join small support groups that meet weekly to encourage each other, share insights, and help one another stay consistent in their learning.

Flexible Class Timings

Understanding that everyone has different schedules, we offer flexible class timings to make it easier to attend regularly. Classes are available in morning, afternoon, and evening slots, so you can choose the time that works best for you.

Join Us on This Journey!

Our Qur'aan classes are more than just memorization—they’re about building a community, fostering dedication, and cultivating a love for the words of Allah. We are excited to help you on your journey, whether it’s strengthening your recitation, deepening your memorization, or simply connecting more closely with the Qur'aan.

Join us at the Quran Reading Club and be part of a journey that not only enhances your Quranic knowledge but also enriches your life through meaningful connections and spiritual growth.
